Change website options

This form allows you to change and remember accessibility and personalisation options on this website

Currently these options allow you to:

  • Enter your name and email
  • Invert all colours across the website
  • Change font colour
  • Change the font
  • Remember all of the above across visits

The above is important namely to allow users to feel welcome and personalise the website as well as being accessable to those unable to read because of the colours or because of the font.
